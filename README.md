# Customer_Segmentation
Customer Segmentation using K-Means Clustering identifying 5 distinct customer groups based on Annual Income and Spending Score. Includes Elbow Method, data scaling, cluster visualization and business interpretation. Dataset: Mall Customers Kaggle.
# Customer Segmentation 🛍️

## Overview
Grouped customers into 5 distinct segments based on Annual Income and Spending Score using K-Means Clustering.

## Customer Segments Identified
| Cluster | Customer Type | Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | 🎯 Average Customers | Maintain engagement |
| 1 | 💰 High Income High Spenders | VIP treatment |
| 2 | ⚠️ Low Income High Spenders | Offer discounts |
| 3 | ✅ Low Income Low Spenders | Budget deals |
| 4 | 💸 High Income Low Spenders | Premium offers |

## Steps Followed
- Exploratory Data Analysis
- Data Scaling
- Elbow Method to find optimal clusters
- K-Means Clustering
- Business Interpretation of Segments
- Visualization of Customer Groups

## Libraries
Python, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Dataset
Mall Customers Dataset - Kaggle
